YOGA CLASSES

You are welcome to attend any yoga class whether you are a beginner or advanced practitioner, because each exercise may be practiced according to the unique capacity of each student. In any case, no exercise should ever cause pain or strain — otherwise, it is a clear sign that one is doing the exercise wrong.



WEIGHT LIFTING

Learn to lift and begin your strength journey. Our Weight lifting classes provide a progressive fundamental instruction focused on technique and form and are good for novice level to advanced and competitive weightlifters.



AEROBIC CLASSES

An aerobics class is a great way to increase your cardiorespiratory capacity! These classes usually last 45 to 60 minutes which is a great goal to set time-wise for cardiorespiratory exercise. Since it takes place in a group atmosphere, chances are you’ll make new friends while you’re at it. If you feel it is something you’ll enjoy, go for it!
